Overview

Fambai CV is an AI-guided, chat-style CV builder for Android. Instead of filling out long forms, you answer simple conversational prompts — and the app builds a structured, professional CV for you.

Key Features

  • Chat-based building — answer questions in a familiar messaging-style interface
  • AI writing assistance — professional wording for skills and experience sections
  • ATS-friendly output — structured to meet modern recruiter expectations
  • Export & share — generate a CV ready to attach to job applications
  • Quick edits — update and tailor your CV for different roles

How It Works

  1. Open the app and begin the CV chat
  2. Answer questions on personal details, education, skills, and experience
  3. The AI organises your answers into a professional layout
  4. Export as a PDF and apply with confidence

Technology

  • Flutter — cross-platform mobile UI
  • Python — AI features and supporting services
