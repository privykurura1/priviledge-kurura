Overview

Fambai CV is an AI-guided, chat-style CV builder for Android. Instead of filling out long forms, you answer simple conversational prompts — and the app builds a structured, professional, ATS-ready CV for you. Designed specifically for the African job market.

Key Features

  • Chat-based building — answer questions in a familiar messaging-style interface
  • AI writing assistance — professional wording for skills and experience sections
  • ATS-friendly output — structured to meet modern recruiter expectations
  • PDF export & share — generate and attach to applications instantly
  • Application letters — role-specific cover letters generated automatically
  • Inline quick edits — update and tailor your CV for different roles

How It Works

  1. Open the app and begin the CV chat session
  2. Answer conversational questions on personal details, education, skills, and experience
  3. The AI organises your answers into a polished, professional layout
  4. Export as PDF and apply with confidence — ATS-ready

Technology

  • Flutter + Provider — cross-platform mobile UI and state management
  • Python — AI features and supporting backend services
  • PDF package — high-quality document generation
  • Google Mobile Ads — sustainable free-tier monetisation
