Fambai CV

Fambai CV is an AI-guided, chat-style CV builder for Android. You answer simple prompts in a conversation and the app generates a structured, professional CV.

Developer
Priviledge Kurura
Platform
Android (Google Play)
Pricing
Free (contains ads)
Best for
Students • Job seekers • Professionals
Key Features
  • Chat-based CV building: answer prompts like WhatsApp style chat.
  • AI writing help: professional wording for skills and experience.
  • ATS-friendly structure: designed to match modern recruiter expectations.
  • Export & share: create a CV for applications (e.g., PDF export).
  • Easy updates: edit quickly for different jobs.
How it works
  1. Open the app and start the CV chat.
  2. Answer questions about personal info, education, skills, and experience.
  3. The AI organizes your details into a professional format.
  4. Export/share and apply for jobs.
Technology
  • Flutter (mobile UI & app)
  • Python (AI features / supporting services)
Privacy & Data Safety
